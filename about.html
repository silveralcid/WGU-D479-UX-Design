About Taniti

A Pacific Hidden Gem

Taniti is a hidden gem in the Pacific, a small but captivating tropical island packed with diverse landscapes and vibrant culture. Despite its modest size of under 500 square miles, Taniti offers everything from sun-kissed sandy beaches to rugged rocky shores, a picturesque harbor, and lush rainforests teeming with life. The island's dramatic mountainous interior is crowned by a small, active volcano, adding an element of adventure to your visit. Home to around 20,000 warm and welcoming locals, Taniti has recently transformed from a fishing and agricultural hub into a burgeoning tourist destination, ready to share its natural wonders with the world.

Safety

The Safest Island Around

Taniti Island is known for its safety and peaceful atmosphere, but as with any destination, it's important to stay vigilant. Recently, we've seen a slight uptick in pickpocketing and other minor incidents. To ensure your stay remains as safe and enjoyable as possible, we encourage you to keep a close eye on your belongings and take common-sense precautions. Your safety is our priority, and a little mindfulness will help you fully enjoy your time on our beautiful island.
